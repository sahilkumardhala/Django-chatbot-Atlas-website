E-GOVERNANCE


E-governance (electronic governance) refers to the use of digital technologies and communication tools to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of government operations and service delivery to citizens. This includes the use of online platforms, mobile applications, social media, and other digital channels to enable citizens to interact with government agencies, access information and services, and participate in decision-making processes. E-governance can also help increase transparency, reduce corruption, and promote accountability in government operations.
